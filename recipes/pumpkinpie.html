Pumpkin Pie

Description

Jesse Szewczyk's take on our basic pumpkin pie adds a complex, autumnal flavor with just four ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 unbaked pie crust
  • 1 can pumpkin puree
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 large eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Fit pie crust into a 9-inch pie dish and place it on a baking sheet.
  2. Make the cheesecake swirl: In a medium, microwave-safe bowl stir together mascarpone, milk, maple syrup, and flour. Microwave on high power until the cheese is completely melted, 20 to 30 seconds. Stir the mixture until completely smooth. (This will make swirling easier.)
